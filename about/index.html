About me

No, I’m not an actual cartoon turtle-looking character.

Anywho, the name’s Ivo. The Amazing Ivo. Remember it well. Welcome to my bolg.

Now, you may be wondering: “Why the hell bolg? Is that a typo? Is it misspelled intentionally? Is there a lore reason for why The Great Ivo is stupid?” Well, allow me to share some brief history on this website. As you may (have) come to learn, I was a student at the totally badass ETH Zurich. Cool place, they gave me a free WordPress blog licence. So one afternoon, while being tormented in class by discrete mathematics, I created The Great Ivo Blog. It was silly, absurd, and relied heavily on pre-made themes. Kind of like this one. So next week I went ahead and showed it to a classmate. He laughed plenty, and we paid no attention to class that morning, and even though he had his own dumb website, he made his own blog. Which he named bolg. This site is in memory of that classmate. May he rest in peace every night when he goes to sleep.

So who am I?

Weren’t you paying attention? I am Ivo the Awesome. First of my name. I was born in a hospital somewhere in country somewhere on this planet – the country you may deduce through my somewhat unique name. There I grew up and did things, before moving with my parents (willingly) to another country (you may deduce it’s Switzerland) and continuing my growing up and doing things there instead. I sort of realized I liked videogames and lazying around, but because those don’t pay the bills I was forced by le society to fully grow up, become an adult, and study something that will one day hopefully pay off.

Otherwise there’s not much to write about myself. At a first glance it may appear that this bogl is very… dedicated to my own person. But I was taught to never give out personal information to strangers online. I suppose that’s how I ended up with such a low digital footprint when I was younger. Not to say that’s a bad thing! Although I must begin making up for it now. So there you go! My full name is Ivo MiriÄ‡ Ribeiro, and my address is [REDACTED].

Have fun lurking in this dumb bolg. Don’t forget to LIKE AND SUBSCRIBE FOR A COOKIE.
